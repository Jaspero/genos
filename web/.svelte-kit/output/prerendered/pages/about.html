About

We are the largest and most advanced laboratory dedicated to glycan analysis, setting the standard in high-throughput glycomics research. With the capacity for over 150,000 glycan analyses each year, our laboratory handles the majority of global glycan research.

Our journey started in 2008 where we analyzed over 1,000 individuals from the Croatian island Vis. More than 10 years later we have published over 150 research publications and completed more than 20 collaborative projects with research and commercial facilities around the world.

Today the laboratory has the capacity for 150,000+ glycan analyses per annum and represents the majority of the glycan research in the world.

We are the world’s leading glycomic lab, with over 20 years of experience in glycomics. Our team of highly qualified experts can help bring your ideas to life.
