Quick rules

• dSpCas9 works with all ED domains
• dSaCas9 works only with: DNMT3A, TET1, VPR, KRAB
• gRNA: unlimited number can be added
• Custom option for each module is available - write your own module of interest, target gene or UCSC coordinates for gRNA design.
